üå± Your Growth Plan: Foundation First

Before you grow, let's make sure your foundation is solid!

1

Perfect Your Product

Make sure your product is consistently great before you try to sell more of it.

  • Get feedback from 5-10 customers
  • Make each item the same quality
  • Write down your process so you don't forget
2

Know Your Numbers

You can't grow if you don't know if you're making money!

  • Track every cost (use our calculator!)
  • Make sure your prices give you profit
  • Record every sale
3

Build Your Customer Base

Happy customers come back AND tell friends!

  • Ask customers to tell others about you
  • Start collecting customer contact info
  • Thank every customer personally

üåø Your Growth Plan: Expand Smart

You've got the basics down. Time to expand carefully!

1

Add a New Product

Expand your line to give customers more options.

  • Add ONE new product that complements your current one
  • Use the same ingredients/supplies if possible (saves money!)
  • Test with existing customers first
2

Find a New Sales Channel

Sell in one more place to reach new customers.

  • If selling to neighbors ‚Üí try a farmers market
  • If at markets ‚Üí try local stores (consignment)
  • Try online with parent help (Instagram, Etsy)
3

Create Repeat Customers

It's easier to sell to someone who already bought from you!

  • Start a simple loyalty program ("Buy 5, get 1 free")
  • Collect phone numbers/emails to let them know about new products
  • Remember regulars and their preferences

üí° Growth Tip

Raise your prices by 10-15% before expanding. It's easier than you think, and it gives you more profit to invest in growth!

üå≥ Your Growth Plan: Scale Up!

You're crushing it! Here's how to grow without burning out.

1

Work Smarter, Not Harder

Find ways to make more without spending more time.

  • Buy supplies in bulk for discounts
  • Make bigger batches at once
  • Create systems (same process every time)
  • Raise prices if demand is high
2

Consider Help

Maybe it's time to get some assistance!

  • Can a sibling help with packaging?
  • Can parents help at busy markets?
  • Can you teach a friend your process?
3

Think About Wholesale

Selling to stores means bigger orders!

  • Stores buy at half your retail price
  • You sell more at once (less work per sale)
  • Start with ONE local store
4

Plan for Busy Seasons

Prepare ahead for your peak selling times.

  • Stock up on supplies before holidays
  • Start making inventory early
  • Maybe skip slow seasons to rest!
